Aftercare

Protect your investment

Keep it clean

Gently wash your tattoo 2–3 times a day with lukewarm water and a fragrance-free, antibacterial soap. Pat dry with a clean paper towel — never rub.

Let it breathe

Remove the initial wrap after 2–4 hours. If a second-skin bandage was applied, leave it on for 3–5 days. Avoid re-wrapping unless advised.

Moisturise daily

Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturiser (Bepanthen or a fragrance-free lotion) 2–3 times a day. Don't over-apply — a little goes a long way.

Avoid the sun

Keep your new tattoo out of direct sunlight for at least 4 weeks. Once healed, always apply SPF 50+ to protect the colour and detail long-term.

No swimming

Avoid pools, the sea, and hot tubs for at least 3–4 weeks. Prolonged water exposure can draw out ink and cause infection before the skin has fully healed.

Don't pick or scratch

Peeling and itching are normal. Picking or scratching can pull out ink and cause scarring. If it itches badly, gently tap — never scratch.

Healing timeline

Days 1–3

Redness, swelling, and some oozing is normal. Clean gently and keep moist. This is the most sensitive phase.

Days 4–7

Peeling begins. The outer skin starts to shed. Keep moisturising and resist the urge to pick.

Week 2–3

The surface appears healed but deeper layers are still recovering. Continue care and sun avoidance.

Month 1–2

Full healing is complete. The tattoo settles, colours become true. Time to show it off.
